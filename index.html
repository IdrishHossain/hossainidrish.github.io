Hi, I'm Hossain Idrish

I am a PhD researcher in Spatially Integrated Social Science at the University of Toledo, Ohio, USA. My research focuses on geospatial analysis, remote sensing, urbanization, conservation, and environmental dynamics using satellite data, UAV technology, and GIS tools.

I have extensive experience in field data collection, spectral analysis, and geospatial research across Bangladesh, Missouri, and Ohio. I am passionate about applying spatial science to solve real-world environmental and social challenges.
